Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 23, 2025

At MultiLangSetu, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information while you use our website.

1. Use of Cookies

We use only session-based cookies to enhance user experience. These cookies are temporary and are automatically deleted when you close your browser tab or window. No persistent or third-party tracking cookies are used.

2. What We Store

We only store the four language selections you make — one source language and up to three target languages — in the session cookies. These preferences help us maintain continuity if you refresh the page, but they are never saved or shared beyond your current session.

3. Google Advertisements

Our website displays advertisements served through Google Ads. These ads may be personalized based on your activity and interests, in accordance with your Google Ad Settings. We do not collect, store, or use any personal information for advertising purposes.

4. Data Storage and Usage

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). The text you input for translation is sent securely to the translation engine and discarded immediately after the translation is completed.

5. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
